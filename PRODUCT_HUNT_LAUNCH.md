## 📝 FULL PRODUCT DESCRIPTION (for PH page)

**Name:** PulseWatch  
**Tagline:** Never miss when your competitors move  
**Category:** AI • Developer Tools • Business Software

**2-sentence description:**
PulseWatch monitors your competitors' websites and uses AI to summarize what changed and why it matters — delivering daily digests to Discord so growth teams never miss a move.

**Extended description:**

Every week, growth teams waste hours manually checking competitor websites. They miss pricing changes, new product launches, and career page updates — until a competitor's sales team tells them first.

PulseWatch fixes this.

**How it works:**
1. Add any competitor URL
2. We scrape daily, diff the changes, and our AI summarizes what matters
3. One daily digest lands in your Discord channel

**What makes us different:**
- 🧠 **AI Intelligence** — Not just "page changed" — we summarize and score importance
- 🔕 **Zero Noise** — Daily digest, not a flood of notifications  
- 👥 **Team-Ready** — Discord-native, your whole team stays informed
- 🔒 **Privacy-First** — Each user configures their own Discord webhook

**Pricing:**
- Free: 3 URLs, daily digest
- Pro: $19/mo, 20 URLs, AI summaries, hourly checks
